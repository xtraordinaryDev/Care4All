Testing Services

  • Drug and alcohol testing (DOT-compliant and non-DOT)
  • DNA testing for legal and personal verification
  • Blood collection and specimen processing for labs or providers

DOT testing is federally regulated and used for transportation or safety-sensitive roles. Non-DOT is common for general employment or personal screening and is more flexible in requirements.

Appointments are recommended to avoid wait times. Walk-ins may be accepted based on staff availability.

All results are delivered via our secure online patient portal. You'll receive access before your appointment. Most results are ready within 24 to 72 hours, depending on the test.

  • A valid photo ID
  • Any required documentation (e.g., employer orders)
  • Follow pre-appointment instructions (such as fasting, if applicable), which will be emailed or texted to you

Phlebotomy Training

Our training is currently available to businesses, clinics, and partner organizations only. We do not offer public individual enrollment.

Graduates receive a completion certificate and are eligible to pursue NHA national certification. We are certified exempt by the Office of Higher Education and are an NHA-recognized training and testing site.
