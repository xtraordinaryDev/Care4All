Our Story

From Lab Services to Real-Time Diagnostics

CareXpedite originated as Care4All Lab Services, offering CLIA-certified drug, alcohol, and DNA testing services. While the foundation of what we do hasn't changed, our technology and speed have.

Now rebranded as CareXpedite, we've evolved from a traditional collection site to a diagnostic-forward company using tools like i-STAT Alinity, which delivers blood test results within 10 minutes. We've minimized outsourcing, increased accuracy, and drastically reduced turnaround time.

"We never stopped doing what we do best—collect and deliver results. We just found a faster, more efficient way to make it happen."

— Coa Murrell, Training Coordinator

Our Mission

To provide accessible, accurate, and timely diagnostic services that serve both the public and business sectors through innovative, tech-integrated solutions.

Our Vision

To redefine what efficient diagnostics looks like—empowering employers, individuals, and training partners with faster testing, expanded collection, and responsive service.

Our Journey So Far

2021
Launch of Care4All Lab Services

Founded with a focus on drug and alcohol testing as a CLIA-certified diagnostic service provider.

2022
DNA Testing Expansion

Added a full range of legal and non-legal DNA testing services for public and business use.

2023
Phlebotomy Training Certified

Became a certified NHA testing and training location. Registered exempt through the Office of Higher Education.

2024
Rebranded as CareXpedite

Refocused on fast diagnostics and AI-enhanced coordination tools.

2025
i-STAT Alinity Launch

Mobile, real-time blood processing goes live — delivering results in under 10 minutes.
